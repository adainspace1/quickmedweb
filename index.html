About Us

Executive QuickMed is a trailblazing medical emergency response service committed to tackling the critical challenge of delayed access to emergency care. Through seamless integration of state-of-the-art GPS technology and a comprehensive suite of services, QuickMed offers users unparalleled access to swift, effective, and efficient medical assistance within an impressive 2 to 5-minute response time. With a robust ecosystem that encompasses e-consultations, ambulance services, medical facility connections, and the revolutionary QuickMed Assist transportation model, we are poised to set new standards in global emergency healthcare

The service is targeted at those in need of quick emergency response, the rich, the poor, the middle-classed technology savvy populace. As a result of poor service delivery experienced by these persons in emergency and to reduce mortality rate of patients QuickMed was created to cater for these persons’
needs.
QuickMed also seeks to provide a platform where those with vehicles and are available to use their vehicles can become Private Ambulance Service Providers to foster the mantra, “Save a Life First”
